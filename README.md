# CampusFix

### **Scalable Complaint Management & Analytics System**

[cite_start]**CampusFix** is an AI-powered platform designed to transform disorganized campus maintenance into a transparent, data-driven operation[cite: 36, 68]. [cite_start]It replaces informal reporting methods like WhatsApp and paper slips with a closed-loop system that ensures accountability from submission to resolution[cite: 55, 71, 72].

---

## **🚀 The Problem**
[cite_start]Traditional campus reporting suffers from the **"Hidden Cost of Chaos"**[cite: 52, 53]:
* [cite_start]**Scattered Reporting:** Issues are lost in verbal notes or informal groups, making them impossible to track[cite: 55].
* [cite_start]**Zero Accountability:** Students lack visibility into the resolution process, leading to a loss of trust[cite: 57].
* [cite_start]**Administrative Overload:** Staff spend approximately 2 hours daily manually sorting and forwarding complaints[cite: 63].
* [cite_start]**The "Band-Aid" Cycle:** Recurring faults are treated as new issues, wasting budgets on repetitive, temporary repairs[cite: 58].

## **💡 Our Solution**
[cite_start]CampusFix provides a centralized dashboard and AI engine to shift campus management from "reactive" fixing to "proactive" asset replacement[cite: 66, 75].

### **Core Features**
* [cite_start]**AI Auto-Categorizer:** Utilizes **Claude AI** to instantly tag complaints by type (e.g., plumbing, electrical) and urgency in under 3 seconds[cite: 74, 79].
* [cite_start]**Legitimacy Scoring:** Filters reports based on specificity, photo evidence, and reporter trust to reduce vague or duplicate complaints[cite: 39, 80].
* [cite_start]**Live Status Tracker:** A "food-delivery style" interface for students to track updates from 'Submitted' to 'Resolved' with photo proof[cite: 72, 81].
* [cite_start]**Admin Command Center:** A unified dashboard to assign tasks, monitor SLA breaches (4-day limit), and track staff performance[cite: 82].
* [cite_start]**Recurring Fault Detection:** Automatically flags assets that fail 3+ times within 60 days, providing Al-generated maintenance notes for facilities heads[cite: 87, 88].

## **📈 Impact & Scalability**
* [cite_start]**Financial Efficiency:** Identifies when asset replacement is cheaper than another repair, saving long-term institutional budgets[cite: 40, 89].
* [cite_start]**Institutional Health Map:** Visualizes high-density issue zones to help administrators prioritize campus-wide renovations[cite: 98].
* [cite_start]**Performance Benchmarking:** Provides data-backed ratings for maintenance staff to optimize campus operations[cite: 97].
* [cite_start]**Scalable Architecture:** Designed to scale easily from a single hostel block to entire university infrastructure[cite: 95].

## **🛠️ Tech Stack**
* [cite_start]**AI Engine:** Claude AI (Categorization & Prioritization)[cite: 79].
* [cite_start]**Analytics:** Predictive modules for fault detection and maintenance optimization[cite: 40, 87].
* [cite_start]**Interface:** Real-time tracking and Admin Dashboards[cite: 39, 81].
