# CampusFix: Scalable Complaint Management & Analytics System

**CampusFix** is an AI-powered platform designed to transform disorganized campus maintenance into a transparent, data-driven operation. It replaces informal reporting methods like WhatsApp, verbal notes, and paper slips with a closed-loop system that ensures accountability from submission to resolution.

# CampusFix 🚀

🔗 Live Demo:[ CampusFix ](https://campusfixprototype.netlify.app/)

A campus issue reporting system that allows students to report and track problems efficiently.

---

## **🚀 The Problem: The Hidden Cost of Chaos**
Traditional campus reporting suffers from systemic inefficiencies:
* **Informal & Scattered Reporting:** Issues are currently reported via unorganized channels, making them impossible to track.
* **Zero Accountability:** Students have no visibility into the resolution process, leading to a total lack of trust.
* **Administrative Overload:** Wardens spend approximately 2 hours daily manually sorting and forwarding complaints.
* **The "Band-Aid" Cycle:** Recurring faults are treated as new issues every time, wasting maintenance budgets on repetitive, temporary repairs.
* **Delayed Action:** Critical infrastructure failures get buried under minor requests due to a lack of automated prioritization.

## **💡 The Solution**
CampusFix provides a centralized dashboard and AI engine to shift campus management from "reactive" fixing to "proactive" asset replacement.

### **Core Features**
* **AI Auto-Categorizer:** Instantly tags complaints by type (e.g., plumbing, electrical) and urgency using **Claude AI** in under 3 seconds.
* **Legitimacy Scoring:** Filters reports based on specificity, photo evidence, and reporter trust to reduce "vague" or duplicate complaints.
* **Live Status Tracker:** A "food-delivery style" interface for students to see updates from 'Submitted' to 'Resolved' with photo proof.
* **Admin Dashboard:** A unified command center to assign tasks, monitor SLA breaches (4-day limit), and track staff performance.
* **Predictive Insights:** Automatically flags assets (e.g., a specific fan or motor) that fail 3+ times within 60 days.

## **📈 Impact & Scalability**
* **Cost Efficiency:** Identifies when a replacement is cheaper than another repair, saving long-term institutional budgets.
* **Institutional Health Map:** Visualizes high-density issue zones to help admins prioritize renovations.
* **Performance Benchmarking:** Provides data-backed ratings for maintenance staff to optimize operations.
* **Campus-Wide Expansion:** Designed to scale easily from a single hostel block to the entire university infrastructure.

## **🛠️ The Engine**
* **AI/ML:** Claude AI for categorization and prioritization.
* **Analytics:** Predictive maintenance alerts and automated triage.
* **Verification:** Closed-loop system ensuring every stakeholder is held accountable through photo-backed closure.
